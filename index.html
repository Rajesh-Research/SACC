Sports Data Analytics Certification Course

offered by
CoE- Sports Analytics and Management, Woxsen University, India
&
Porto Business School, University of Porto, Portugal

This comprehensive program combines cutting-edge data science techniques with real-world sports analytics applications to prepare professionals for the future of sports management. Participants will master advanced machine learning, AI algorithms, and performance metrics while gaining hands-on experience through industry partnerships and practical case studies.

Course offered in Online and Hybrid modes

🌐
Online Mode
Complete flexibility with live interactive sessions, recorded lectures, and virtual collaboration tools
🌍
Hybrid Mode
Online learning combined with intensive in-person experience in Porto, Portugal
